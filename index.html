Instructions

Three rounds of the following:

  1. 30 deep breaths.
  2. Full exhale + hold your breath.
  3. Full inhale + hold your breath for 15 seconds.

Warning

Please ensure you are sitting or laying down in a safe environment before practicing. Do not practice any breathing exercises in a place or posisiton that could cause harm should you become light headed or faint.

If you feel light headed, stop.
